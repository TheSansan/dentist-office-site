Contact Us

Falls Park Dentistry offers extended hours to provide greater flexibility in scheduling appointments around your work and school schedule. We are open for morning, lunch, and evening appointments. Use the link below to send an appointment request to our office or call us at 864-243-8371. We will contact you as soon as possible to schedule your appointment. For your added convenience, we validate parking.

Appointments & Cancellations

When we make your appointment, we are reserving a room for your particular needs. We ask that if you must change an appointment to please give us at least 24 business hours notice. This courtesy makes it possible to give your reserved room to another patient who would like it.
Repeated cancellations or missed appointments will result in loss of future appointment privileges.
We feel that our patient's time is valuable. We ask that you show up on time for your appointment. When your appointment is made, a room is reserved, your records are prepared, and special instruments are readied for your visit. Except for emergency treatment for another patient, you can expect us to be prompt. We, of course, would appreciate the same courtesy from you.
